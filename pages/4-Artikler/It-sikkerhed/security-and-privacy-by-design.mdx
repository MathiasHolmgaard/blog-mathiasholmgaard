## Security by Design

Security by Design, eller sikkerhed ved design, er et framework til udvikling af IT-systemer, hvor sikkerhedstiltag og -funktioner tænkes ind fra starten af designprocessen. Dette betyder, at sikkerhed bliver en integreret del af systemet og ikke blot en eftertanke eller en reaktiv tilgang.

Det giver også en fælles måde at beskytte systemer på således at kan lette samarbejdet med andre udviklere om at bygge mere sikre systemer.

Fordelen ved Security by Design er, at det giver mulighed for at identificere og løse potentielle sikkerhedsrisici tidligt i udviklingsprocessen. Ved at tænke sikkerhed ind fra starten kan man minimere risikoen for sårbarheder og angreb, da man allerede har implementeret de nødvendige sikkerhedsforanstaltninger.

En af de vigtigste principper inden for Security by Design er "defense in depth", hvilket betyder at man implementerer flere lag af sikkerhedstiltag for at beskytte systemet. Dette inkluderer blandt andet adgangskontrol, kryptering, sikkerhedskopiering og overvågning.

Nøgleprincipperne består af:

1.	Threat Modeling: Identify and understand potential threats early in the development process.
2.	Secure Coding Practices: Follow best practices for writing secure code, such as input validation, proper error handling, and avoiding common vulnerabilities like SQL injection and cross-site scripting (XSS).
3.	Least Privilege: Grant the minimum level of access necessary for users and systems to perform their functions.
4.	Defense in Depth: Implement multiple layers of security controls to protect against threats.
5.	Regular Security Testing: Conduct regular security assessments, such as code reviews, penetration testing, and vulnerability scanning.
6.	Incident Response Planning: Prepare for potential security incidents with a well-defined response plan.





Privacy and security by design are principles that emphasize integrating privacy and security measures into the development process from the very beginning, rather than as an afterthought. Here’s a breakdown of each concept:

## Privacy by Design
Privacy by design is a framework that ensures privacy is embedded into the design and architecture of IT systems and business practices. It involves:
1.	Proactive not Reactive; Preventative not Remedial: Anticipate and prevent privacy-invasive events before they happen.
2.	Privacy as the Default Setting: Ensure personal data is automatically protected in any IT system or business practice.
3.	Privacy Embedded into Design: Integrate privacy into the design and architecture of IT systems and business practices.
4.	Full Functionality: Achieve both privacy and security without compromising functionality.
5.	End-to-End Security: Ensure data is securely managed throughout its lifecycle.
6.	Visibility and Transparency: Maintain transparency about data practices and ensure they are verifiable.
7.	Respect for User Privacy: Keep user privacy paramount by offering strong privacy defaults, appropriate notice, and user-friendly options.

## Security by Design
Security by design involves incorporating security measures into the development lifecycle to protect systems and data from threats. Key principles include:
1.	Threat Modeling: Identify and understand potential threats early in the development process.
2.	Secure Coding Practices: Follow best practices for writing secure code, such as input validation, proper error handling, and avoiding common vulnerabilities like SQL injection and cross-site scripting (XSS).
3.	Least Privilege: Grant the minimum level of access necessary for users and systems to perform their functions.
4.	Defense in Depth: Implement multiple layers of security controls to protect against threats.
5.	Regular Security Testing: Conduct regular security assessments, such as code reviews, penetration testing, and vulnerability scanning.
6.	Incident Response Planning: Prepare for potential security incidents with a well-defined response plan.

## Privacy by Design

Privacy by Design, eller privatliv ved design, handler om at tænke beskyttelse af personlige oplysninger ind i designet af IT-systemer. Dette indebærer at sikre, at der bliver taget hensyn til privatlivets fred og beskyttelse af personlige oplysninger allerede fra starten af udviklingsprocessen.

Ved at implementere Privacy by Design kan man sikre, at brugernes personlige oplysninger bliver behandlet på en sikker og fortrolig måde. Dette kan omfatte at indsamle kun de nødvendige oplysninger, at anvende stærk kryptering, at give brugerne kontrol over deres data og at have klare retningslinjer for databehandling og -opbevaring.

Fordelen ved Privacy by Design er, at det skaber tillid hos brugerne og beskytter deres privatliv. Ved at tænke privatlivet ind fra starten af designprocessen kan man undgå potentielle brud på personlige oplysninger og overholde gældende databeskyttelseslovgivning.

Samlet set er både Security by Design og Privacy by Design vigtige tilgange til at sikre IT-systemers sikkerhed og beskyttelse af personlige oplysninger. Ved at tænke sikkerhed og privatliv ind fra planlægning og design kan man minimere risikoen for angreb og overtrædelser af privatlivet, samtidig med at man skaber tillid hos brugerne.
